Oct. 10, 2017
9:18 AM

As the public face and pugnacious spirit behind his company, Harvey Weinstein made himself synonymous with a kind of prestige cinema that was designed to attract discerning audiences and scoop up Oscars by the armful.

That is, until October 2017, when explosive allegations surfaced about decades of sexual harassment and misconduct by Weinstein. The claims against the longtime movie mogul soon expanded to dozens of women within the industry, ultimately bringing down Weinstein and his company in addition to sparking movements such as #MeToo and #TimesUp. Take a look back at the stories that shaped one of the fastest downfalls in recent Hollywood history.
