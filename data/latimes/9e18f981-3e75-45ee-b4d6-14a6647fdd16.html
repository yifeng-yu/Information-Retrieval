Your 2019 guide to

ice cream and gelato

in and around Los Angeles

by Amy Scattergood

July 30, 2019, 12:05 a.m.

It is a strange and marvelous time to be an ice cream fan. There are more excellent shops making ice cream and gelato than ever, sourcing ingredients from local strawberry fields and Italian pistachio orchards, churning the stuff with wooden paddles, and blasting it with liquid nitrogen. Stranger Things is now set in a scoops shop and folks are licking pints in grocery stores. (Lick your own ice cream. Licking somebody elses is gross, and apparently a felony.)

Restaurants are upping their own game: A glass of strawberry ice cream at the recently opened Los Angeles restaurant Antico was the best thing Ive eaten in a very long time. And if you havent eaten your way through Mozzas gelato desserts, its about time. Its going to be a long, hot summer, so here are 50 places in L.A. and beyond to get some excellent scoops.
